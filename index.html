Full Stack Developer!

I am a 2nd year student majoring in web design, with 3 months of work experience in this field. My passion lies in creating engaging websites and implementing them through front end development. I pride myself on staying up to date with current design trends and leveraging my creativity to create user-friendly and visually interesting websites. During my studies, I developed a deep understanding of Java programming principles,understanding of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) principles.
